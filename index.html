Light Bulb Game

Can you find out how 3 mysterious light bulbs work together?

  • Intro
  • Practice
  • Puzzles

Intro

In this game, you can use your mouse to control 3 light bulbs:
  • To turn on a light bulb, click it on the screen
  • To turn off a light bulb, just click on it again

Practice

Some light bulbs can give light to others. When these special light bulbs turn on, they can turn on other light bulbs at the same time! — Let's see how this works!

Example 1

Press the space bar to see which light bulb can give light to another.

Example 2

Sometimes more than one light bulb can give light to others. Press the space bar again and you will see a new arrow.

Example 3

Light bulbs can work in different ways. Press the space bar to see another example.

Example 4

They can work in many more ways. Press the space bar to see one last example.

Congratulations, you've learned a lot about light bulbs! Ready to solve some puzzles?

Puzzles

Sometimes we don't know how the light bulbs work together because the arrows are hidden away. You have to figure it out! Good news: In each puzzle, the three light bulbs can only work in one of two ways.

How to solve a puzzle? You can turn on only one light bulb and see what happens to the other two. If you choose a helpful light bulb, it will tell you the correct answer!
